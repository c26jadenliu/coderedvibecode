There’s a place here for you.

Whether you’re a casual gamer, competitive player, streamer, or supporter, you can plug in at a pace that makes sense for you. The live ProjectCodeRed.com page spotlights ways to help: donating hardware, sponsoring connectivity, moderating safe spaces, or hosting a charity stream.

Help with community management, operations, outreach, or sponsor an event as an individual, team, or brand.
