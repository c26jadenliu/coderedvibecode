About

A nonprofit built at the intersection of gaming and service.

Project Code Red exists to make it easier for gamers, streamers, and supporters to turn their communities into a force for good. The flagship site spotlights our focus on digital equity, mental wellness, and building safer online spaces alongside direct community relief.

Status
Federally recognized 501(c)(3)

We operate as a tax-exempt public charity, with a focus on transparency and practical, measurable impact.

Mission

Why Project Code Red exists

We want gaming communities to be places where people are seen, supported, and given better access to opportunities and technology.

What we believe

We believe that games are more than entertainment. They are shared spaces where friendships form, support networks grow, and ideas spread quickly.

When those spaces are intentionally inclusive and purpose-driven, they can move people to take action in the real world.

How we show up

We keep things simple: low drama, high accountability, and a bias toward doing the unglamorous work that makes good events, good streams, and good partnerships possible.

We work with creators, teams, and partners who share that mindset.

Priorities

Guided by what matters on ProjectCodeRed.com

The live site emphasizes three promises: protect the vibe, create real-world access, and celebrate every person who shows up to serve.

Protect the vibe

Safety, moderation, and empathy are built into our Discord, streams, and events. We keep conversations healthy so everyone can participate.

Create access

We chase practical outcomes—devices for students, connectivity for families, and bridges to mental health resources curated with partners.

Celebrate service

From shout-outs to volunteer credits, we champion the people who give their time, energy, and creativity to make every activation count.
