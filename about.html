About

A nonprofit built at the intersection of gaming and service.

Project Code Red exists to make it easier for gamers, streamers, and supporters to turn their communities into a force for good.

Status
Federally recognized 501(c)(3)

We operate as a tax-exempt public charity, with a focus on transparency and practical, measurable impact.

Mission

Why Project Code Red exists

We want gaming communities to be places where people are seen, supported, and given better access to opportunities and technology.

What we believe

We believe that games are more than entertainment. They are shared spaces where friendships form, support networks grow, and ideas spread quickly.

When those spaces are intentionally inclusive and purpose-driven, they can move people to take action in the real world.

How we show up

We keep things simple: low drama, high accountability, and a bias toward doing the unglamorous work that makes good events, good streams, and good partnerships possible.

We work with creators, teams, and partners who share that mindset.
